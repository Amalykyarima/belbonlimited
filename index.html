BELBON SUGAR PLC

Belbon Industries Limited is a diversified and fully integrated conglomerate with an annual group turnover in excess of US$4 billion (2016) with vibrant operations in Nigeria and Africa across a wide range of sectors including cement, sugar, salt, condiments, packaging, energy, port operations, fertilizer, and petrochemicals. Our core business focus is to provide local, value-added products and services that meet the ‘basic needs’ of the populace through the construction and operation of large scale manufacturing facilities in Nigeria and across Africa. We are focused on building local manufacturing capacity to generate employment, reduce capital flight and increase local value addition.

MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to make overwhelming positive impact in the lives of people through efficient delivery of products, goods and services and making deliberate positive efforts in making the world a better place.

OUR VISION

We dream a world where our brand will be a household name and lives are positively changed through the creation of jobs and strengthening of the economy.
